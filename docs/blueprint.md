# **App Name**: SereneQuote

## Core Features:

- Daily Quote Delivery: Deliver a new, carefully selected inspirational quote each day.
- Favorite Quote Saving: Allow users to save their favorite quotes for later viewing.
- Quote Sharing: Enable users to share quotes with friends via social media or messaging apps.
- Quote Library Browsing: Provide a library of past quotes that users can browse.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke tranquility and peace. It adds a touch of sophistication while remaining calming.
- Background color: Warm cream (#F5F5DC) to provide a soft, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#D4A373), an analogous color to teal, but brighter and less saturated.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a transitional serif with a literary, vintage, slightly formal feel, providing readability and a touch of elegance for quotes and UI text.
- Use minimalist icons with rounded edges to represent actions and categories, maintaining a gentle and approachable feel.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout with plenty of whitespace to enhance readability and promote a sense of calm.
- Use subtle, gentle animations for transitions and interactions, avoiding anything jarring or distracting.
